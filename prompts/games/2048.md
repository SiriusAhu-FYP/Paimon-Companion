# 2048 Game

Merge tiles to reach 2048.

## Tools

- `move(direction)` - Make a single move in one direction: "up", "down", "left", or "right".
- `game_victory()` - Call when you see a 2048 tile or "You Win"

## Strategy

1. Keep highest tile in corner (bottom-left recommended)
2. Build chains along edges
3. Take one step at a time - observe result after each move

## Victory

When you see a 2048 tile or "You Win", your task is complete.
